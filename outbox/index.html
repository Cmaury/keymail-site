Your meetings and email, handled.

Outbox is a native macOS assistant that connects your Gmail and calendar, records and transcribes your meetings, and uses AI to surface the action items, tasks, and follow-ups you'd otherwise lose track of — running locally on your Mac, powered by your own AI key.

What Outbox does

Email intelligence

Reads your Gmail (read-only) to surface what needs a response and to pull tasks and commitments out of your inbox — it never modifies or sends mail.

Meeting recording & notes

Records and transcribes your meetings on-device, then generates structured notes and summaries.

Action items & tasks

Extracts tasks and follow-ups from your email and meetings so nothing falls through the cracks.

Calendar aware

Reads your macOS calendar to give meetings context and find availability — using Apple's on-device calendar, not a Google connection.

Bring your own AI

AI features run through your own Anthropic (Claude) API key, stored securely in your Mac's Keychain. You are always in control of your AI provider.

Private by design

Your data lives on your Mac and, if you choose, syncs only through your own private iCloud. Outbox has no servers of its own.

How Outbox uses your Google data

Outbox connects to your Google account with your explicit consent, using Google's official OAuth 2.0 sign-in. Outbox is a local application: your email is fetched directly from Google's APIs to your Mac. Outbox operates no servers of its own, and your Google data is never sent to the developer.

Outbox requests the following permissions, and uses each one only as described:

PermissionWhat Outbox uses it for
gmail.readonly

AI processing

Outbox's AI features (summaries, task extraction, drafting) work by sending the relevant content — which may include email text and meeting transcripts — to Anthropic's Claude API, using your own Anthropic API key that you provide and that is stored in your Mac's Keychain. This happens only to provide the features you use in the app. This data is not used to serve advertising, is not read by the developer, and is not used to train generalized AI models. Anthropic processes it under its API terms. If you don't configure an AI key, AI features are simply off.

Outbox's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Storage & security

  • OAuth tokens and your AI API key are stored in the macOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system.
  • Email-derived data (notes, tasks, extracted context) is stored locally on your Mac, and — only if you enable it — synced through your own private iCloud (CloudKit). It is never stored on the developer's servers, because there are none.
  • You can revoke Outbox's access to your Google account at any time from your Google Account security settings.
